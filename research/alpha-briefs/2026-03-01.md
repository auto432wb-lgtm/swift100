# Weekly Alpha Brief — 2026-03-01

## Macro / Narrative Snapshot

- Institutional appetite for tokenized RWAs (especially treasury rails) remains a strong TradFi-to-onchain bridge.
- AI token performance is selective; quality + liquidity matter more than narrative alone.
- Stable-yield demand is strong, but downside tails sit in exits, governance, and issuer concentration.

## Top 3 Opportunities

### 1) ONDO (RWA)
- **Thesis:** One of the clearest listed proxies for tokenized treasury / RWA expansion.
- **Catalyst window:** 30–90 days (adoption + integration flow).
- **Score:** 33/40
- **Entry logic:** Scale in tranches around key support.
- **Invalidation:** RWA trend deterioration + sustained relative weakness.
- **Target logic:** Prior range highs, then extension only with confirming volume.
- **Suggested size:** Medium core (start 1.0R, add on confirmation).
- **Confidence:** 7/10

### 2) TAO (AI)
- **Thesis:** Higher-quality AI beta expression when leadership rotates back to liquid names.
- **Catalyst window:** 2–8 weeks.
- **Score:** 31/40
- **Entry logic:** Buy pullbacks into demand zones; avoid chase entries.
- **Invalidation:** Relative-strength failure + lower-high trend structure.
- **Target logic:** Reversion to high-liquidity nodes, then trend continuation if leadership returns.
- **Suggested size:** Satellite sleeve (0.75R start).
- **Confidence:** 6.5/10

### 3) USDY / Tokenized Treasury Sleeve (Stable Yield)
- **Thesis:** Treasury-backed tokenized yield for lower-volatility carry and optionality preservation.
- **Catalyst window:** Quarterly rate-path and adoption trend.
- **Score:** 34/40
- **Entry logic:** Time-based DCA allocation.
- **Invalidation:** Issuer/custody concerns or redemption-confidence deterioration.
- **Target logic:** Yield capture (not momentum trading).
- **Suggested size:** Core defensive sleeve per cash-management rules.
- **Confidence:** 8/10

## Risk Dashboard
- Narrative concentration risk: **High** if overweight RWA + treasury rails.
- Liquidity risk: **Medium** for secondary names.
- Event/unlock risk (30d): Monitor before adding risk.
- Correlation risk to BTC/ETH: Elevated in stress regimes.

## 7-Day Action Plan
1. Complete full scorecards for ONDO, TAO, and stable-yield vehicle.
2. Define exact invalidation levels and size caps before first fill.
3. Reassess end of week: keep / add / cut by thesis integrity + relative strength.

## Failure Conditions (Plan Wrong If...)
- Macro flips risk-off and compresses risk beta broadly.
- RWA/AI narrative persists but price fails to confirm.
- Hidden structural risk emerges in stable-yield wrapper.
