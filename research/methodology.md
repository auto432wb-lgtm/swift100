# Swift 100 Crypto Methodology

## Universe

### Tier 1 (Core)
- RWA tokenization protocols
- AI infra/application tokens with measurable traction
- Stable-yield / tokenized treasury rails

### Tier 2 (Opportunistic)
- Adjacent L2 infra tied to Tier 1 narratives
- New listings with liquidity + clear catalysts

### Tier 3 (Avoid by default)
- Illiquid narrative-only assets
- Weak docs, weak governance, weak transparency

## 40-Point Scorecard

Each asset is scored 0–5 in 8 categories:

1. Narrative strength
2. Revenue / fee reality
3. Liquidity depth
4. Token unlock risk
5. Team credibility
6. Catalyst visibility (30–90d)
7. On-chain activity trend
8. Risk-adjusted upside

### Decision Bands
- **32–40:** Active allocation candidate
- **24–31:** Watchlist + wait for trigger
- **<24:** Pass

## Position Rules

- No position without written thesis
- No position without invalidation
- No position without size plan

### Entry Discipline
- Prefer scaling vs single-fill impulse entries
- Avoid chasing vertical candles

### Exit Discipline
- Thesis break = reduce or exit
- Catalyst completion = de-risk
- Liquidity deterioration = cut size

## Risk Controls

- Cap narrative concentration
- Cap illiquid exposure
- Require pre-trade invalidation before first fill
- Reassess weekly with relative strength + thesis integrity
